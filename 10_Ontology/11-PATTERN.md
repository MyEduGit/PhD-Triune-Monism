## WHAT IS PATTERN?

Pattern is the **non-energy configuration** of reality. It is not itself energy — it is the ARRANGEMENT imposed on energy. Pattern is to energy what form is to matter in Aristotle's metaphysics.

### Key Properties

1. **Pattern is not energy.** You cannot measure pattern with an energy detector. Pattern is the FORM, not the STUFF.
2. **Pattern pervades all domains.** There are material patterns (crystal structures), mental patterns (logical forms), and spiritual patterns (moral laws).
3. **Pattern pervades all levels.** It exists at the finite, absonite, absolute, and infinite levels.
4. **Paradise is the absolute pattern.** The Isle of Paradise is the archetype of all material configuration — the "master blueprint."
5. **Pattern is bestowed by Deity.** It does not emerge randomly — it is imposed by intelligent design (the Father through Paradise, the Son through spiritual pattern, the Spirit through mental pattern).

## THE PATTERN-INFORMATION CONNECTION

Modern physics increasingly treats **information** as fundamental. Wheeler's "it from bit," Tegmark's mathematical universe, IIT's "integrated information" — all point toward pattern/information as a basic category of reality.

The Foreword anticipated this by making pattern a SEPARATE COMPONENT from energy. Reality is not just energy — it is PATTERNED energy. The pattern is as important as the energy.

**PhD connection to IIT:**
Integrated Information Theory (Tononi) says consciousness IS integrated information (phi). The Foreword would say: phi measures one ASPECT of mind pattern — the degree to which mental energy is configured in an integrated way. But pattern alone does not produce consciousness — you also need mind energy (from the Infinite Spirit) and personality (from the Father). IIT gets the pattern right but misses the energy and the personality.
