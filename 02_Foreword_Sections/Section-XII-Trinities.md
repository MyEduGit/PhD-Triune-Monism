## KEY CONCEPTS

### Triunities (Functional Relationships)
Seven triunities — each a functional relationship among three of the seven Absolutes. These are the "laws of nature" at the absolute level:
1. Father + Son + Spirit = Personal Reality
2. Father + Son + Paradise = Spirit-Matter Architecture
3. Father + Spirit + Unqualified Abs = Energy Evolution
4. Father + Son + Deity Abs = Spiritual Creation
5. Father + Paradise + Unqualified Abs = Space and Cosmic Force
6. **Father + Spirit + Deity Abs = Mind-Deity Orientation** (PhD KEY)
7. Father + Deity Abs + Universal Abs = Deity Conservation

### Trinities (Unity-Level Unification)
Three Trinities — super-personal unifications of Deity:
1. **Paradise Trinity** (existential) — Father + Son + Spirit functioning as ONE
2. **Trinity Ultimate** (1st experiential) — Supreme + Creative Corps + Architects of Master Universe
3. **Trinity Absolute** (2nd experiential) — Supreme + Ultimate + Consumator of Universe Destiny

### Trinity of Trinities
The unification of all three Trinities = the THEORY OF EVERYTHING itself. An evolving, open-ended, living unification of all reality.
