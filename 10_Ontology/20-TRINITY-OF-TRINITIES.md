## THE STRUCTURE

The Trinity of Trinities is a three-level meta-Trinity:

```
TRINITY OF TRINITIES
├── Level 1: Paradise Trinity (existential)
│   Father + Son + Spirit
│   → Governs all eternal, existential reality
│
├── Level 2: Trinity Ultimate (first experiential)
│   Supreme + Supreme Creators + Architects
│   → Governs all finite and absonite experiential reality
│
└── Level 3: Trinity Absolute (second experiential)
    Supreme + Ultimate + Consummator
    → Would govern all absolute experiential reality
```

## THE VERDICT

**The Trinity of Trinities IS the Foreword's Theory of Everything** — with two caveats:

### Caveat 1: It is ITSELF evolving
Trinities 2 and 3 are not yet complete. The ToE is a LIVING, GROWING unification — not a static formula. This is actually a STRENGTH: it means the ToE can accommodate new reality without needing revision.

### Caveat 2: The evil/error gap
No Trinity explicitly accounts for how evil, error, or imperfection arise in a reality sourced from perfect Deity. This must be addressed in the dissertation's Chapter 7 (Objections and Responses).
