## KEY CONCEPTS

### The Ultimate
- Deity of the ABSONITE level (transcending time and space without being infinite)
- "Eventuating" — a novel ontological category between existing and not-existing
- Unifies the master universe (all finite creation plus outer space levels)
- Will ultimately represent the unification of the first experiential Trinity

### Absonite Mind
If mind functions at the absonite level, then consciousness CAN FUNCTION WITHOUT MATERIAL MECHANISM. This is a key philosophical claim: mind is not dependent on brains, because mind exists at levels where there are no brains (absonite, absolute, infinite).
