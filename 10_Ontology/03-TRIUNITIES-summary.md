## What Is a Triunity?
A functional (non-personal) association of three of the Seven Absolutes that governs a specific domain of reality. The Father appears in ALL seven. Think of them as the "laws of nature" at the absolute level.

## The Seven Triunities

| # | Members | Name | Domain Governed |
|---|---------|------|-----------------|
| 1 | Father + Son + Spirit | Infinite Actuality | All personal reality |
| 2 | Father + Son + Paradise | Power-Pattern | Spirit-matter architecture |
| 3 | Father + Spirit + Unqualified Abs | Force-Energy Evolution | Energy transmutation |
| 4 | Father + Son + Deity Abs | Spiritual Creation | New beings and spirit realities |
| 5 | Father + Paradise + Unqualified Abs | Reactive Infinity | Space and cosmic force |
| 6 | Father + Spirit + Deity Abs | Mind-Deity Orientation | Mind inherently seeks meaning |
| 7 | Father + Deity Abs + Universal Abs | Deity Unity | Conservation of deity coherence |

## The Seven Laws of the ToE

1. All persons exist through Father-Son-Spirit cooperation
2. Matter exists to serve spirit (Paradise for the Son)
3. Energy evolves from potential to actual through Spirit's manipulation
4. New beings emerge from deity potential, drawn by spiritual gravity
5. Space and cosmic force exist through Paradise gravity + Unqualified reservoir
6. **Mind inherently seeks meaning** (PhD key — Triunity 6)
7. Deity unity is conserved through all change

## 8 Unmentioned Triunities
15 combinations are possible (Father + any 2 of 6). Only 7 are named. The other 8 may be real but unelaborated, or non-functional.

## PhD Key
**Triunity 6** establishes that consciousness is not value-neutral information processing but inherently meaning-seeking. This is the core claim for the Philosophy of Mind thesis.
