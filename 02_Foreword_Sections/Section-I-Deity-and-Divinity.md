## KEY CONCEPTS

### Deity
Deity is the quality of divinity. It functions on multiple levels:
- **Static** — being, existing
- **Potential** — becoming, self-willed
- **Associative** — self-personalized (as in the Trinity)
- **Creative** — self-distributive
- **Evolving** — self-expansive (as in the Supreme)
- **Supreme** — self-experiential
- **Ultimate** — self-projective

**PhD significance:** Deity is NOT identical to God. God is a person; Deity is a quality that persons (and even non-personal entities) can express. This distinction is philosophically important — it separates the PERSONAL from the DIVINE, allowing mind (a non-personal phenomenon) to participate in deity reality through mind gravity.

### Divinity
The quality of Deity as experienced. Characterized by unity (truth), beauty, and goodness. In personality: love, mercy, ministry. In impersonal reality: justice, power, sovereignty.

**PhD significance:** The triad truth-beauty-goodness maps directly onto the three domains: truth (mind), beauty (spirit), goodness (personality/will). This is the VALUES foundation for mind gravity.

### God
Three uses: (1) God the Father, (2) God the Son / God the Spirit (coordinate Deities), (3) God the Supreme / God the Ultimate / God the Absolute (experiential Deities).
