This hierarchy implies that consciousness PARTICIPATES in three levels of experiential Deity:

2. **Every transcendent insight** (worship, wisdom, cosmic consciousness) touches the Ultimate's eventuating
3. **Every absolute value recognized** (truth, beauty, goodness in their purest form) reaches toward the Absolute

**Consciousness is not just aware OF the universe — consciousness BUILDS the experiential Deities.**
