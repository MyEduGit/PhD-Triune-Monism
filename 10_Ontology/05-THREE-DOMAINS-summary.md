## The Three Domains

| Domain | Source | Gravity | Experienced As |
|---|---|---|---|
| Matter | Paradise | Physical | Things, facts |
| Mind | Infinite Spirit | Mind | Meanings, logic |
| Spirit | Eternal Son | Spirit | Values, truth, beauty |

## Why Not 1 or 2 Domains?

- **Physicalism (1):** Cannot explain qualia (hard problem)
- **Dualism (2):** Cannot explain interaction (interaction problem)
- **Idealism (1):** Cannot explain regularity of physics
- **Panpsychism (1):** Cannot explain combination (combination problem)
- **Triune monism (3):** Solves all four problems

## The Core PhD Argument

The Infinite Spirit simultaneously:
1. Sources all mind/consciousness
2. Manipulates physical energy
3. Mediates between spirit and matter

This resolves the interaction problem because the mediator inherently operates in BOTH domains.

## Mind Gravity (Novel Concept)

Consciousness inherently seeks meaning — not by computation but by ontological orientation toward the Infinite Spirit. This explains intentionality, the binding problem, and why consciousness has a teleological character.

## Things-Meanings-Values

A sunset = electromagnetic radiation (matter) + beauty/transition (mind) + awe/worship (spirit). All three are REAL, not three perspectives on one thing.
