## KEY CONCEPTS

### The Universal Father
- First Person of Deity
- **Primal cause** and **upholder** of all reality
- Source of PERSONALITY — the Father alone bestows personality
- Source of WILL — the Father's freewill choice inaugurates all reality
- Exercises physical, mind, and spirit gravity through his Absolutes

### Father's Relationship to the Universe
- Father is PERSONALLY present in Paradise
- Father is SPIRITUALLY present through Thought Adjusters (fragments of his spirit indwelling mortal minds)
- Father relates to all creatures through PERSONALITY bestowal

### The First Source and Center
- Technical name for the Father as the origin of all reality
- Functions in 7 capacities corresponding to the 7 Absolutes
