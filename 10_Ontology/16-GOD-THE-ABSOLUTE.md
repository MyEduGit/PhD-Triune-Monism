## THE THEORETICAL COMPLETION

God the Absolute would be the EXPERIENTIAL equivalent of the I AM — a being who has actualized ALL potentials through experience. If fully actualized:

- The Deity Absolute (all deified potential) would be fully experienced
- The Unqualified Absolute (all undeified potential) would be fully configured
- The Universal Absolute would no longer need to compensate — perfect unity achieved

**The paradox:** Can infinity be fully experienced? If the potentials are truly infinite, God the Absolute may be permanently ASYMPTOTIC — forever approaching completion, never reaching it. This would mean:

> **The Theory of Everything is itself evolving and can never be "finished."**
