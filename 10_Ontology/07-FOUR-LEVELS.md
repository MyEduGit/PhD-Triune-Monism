## THE FOUR LEVELS

### Level 1: FINITE

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Time | YES — exists within time |
| Space | YES — exists within space |
| Beginning/End | YES — has origin and destiny |
| Deity expression | God the Supreme (evolving) |
| Beings | Humans, midwayers, angels, Material Sons |
| Physics domain | Everything physics studies |
| Consciousness | Personal mind with material mechanism (brain) |

**This is the level physics and most philosophy operate on.** Everything with a beginning, everything in space and time, everything that can be measured or experienced by finite creatures — this is the finite level.

**PhD significance:** Most philosophy of mind debates occur entirely at this level. The Foreword claims this is inherently limited — you cannot fully understand consciousness at the finite level because consciousness participates in higher levels (morontia mind, spirit mind).

---

### Level 2: ABSONITE

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Time | TRANSCENDED — not bound by time but not eternal |
| Space | TRANSCENDED — not bound by space but not infinite |
| Beginning/End | TRANSCENDED — no origin or destiny but not unconditioned |
| Deity expression | God the Ultimate (eventuating) |
| Beings | Architects of the Master Universe, Transcendentalers |
| Physics domain | Beyond physics — transcends spacetime |
| Consciousness | Absonite mind — functions without material mechanism |

**The absonite is the intermediate level** between the finite and the absolute. It transcends time and space WITHOUT being infinite or eternal. It is the level at which the master universe is PLANNED — the Architects of the Master Universe operate here.

**Key concept — "eventuating":**
God the Ultimate is not "existing" (that's existential) and not "evolving" (that's experiential-finite). The Ultimate is "eventuating" — a unique verb the Foreword uses for absonite-level actualization. It means: coming into being in a way that transcends time but is not simply eternal.

**PhD significance:** The absonite level suggests that consciousness has capacities BEYOND what the brain mediates. If mind can function "absontely" (without material mechanism), then the physicalist equation of mind with brain activity is fundamentally mistaken — not just incomplete, but wrong in principle.

---

### Level 3: ABSOLUTE

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Time | NO — eternal |
| Space | NO — infinite |
| Beginning/End | NO — unconditioned |
| Deity expression | The Paradise Trinity (existential); God the Absolute (experiential) |
| Beings | The three Deities (Father, Son, Spirit) |
| Physics domain | Utterly beyond physics |
| Consciousness | Absolute consciousness — infinite awareness |

**The absolute level is the level of the eternal Deities themselves.** The Father, Son, and Spirit exist at this level. The Paradise Trinity functions at this level. The three Absolutes (Deity, Unqualified, Universal) hold absolute potential at this level.

**God the Absolute (experiential):** This would be the being who has actualized ALL potential — a complete experiential expression of the absolute. The Foreword implies this may never fully actualize (see [[06-ACTUAL-VS-POTENTIAL]] — potential is infinite and may not be exhaustible).

---

### Level 4: INFINITE

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Time | Beyond category |
| Space | Beyond category |
| Beginning/End | Beyond category |
| Deity expression | The I AM |
| Beings | None — only the I AM |
| Physics domain | Utterly beyond any framework |
| Consciousness | Infinite — undifferentiated awareness containing all |

**The infinite level is the I AM itself.** It is not a "level" in the ordinary sense — it is the ground of all levels. It encompasses and transcends finite, absonite, and absolute simultaneously. No creature can fully comprehend this level. It is the permanent horizon of all cosmic evolution.

## THE RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN LEVELS

The four levels are NOT parallel or independent. They are NESTED:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ INFINITE (The I AM)                                         │
│  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐    │
│  │ ABSOLUTE (Paradise Trinity, the Deities)             │    │
│  │  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐    │    │
│  │  │ ABSONITE (God the Ultimate, Transcendentalers)    │    │
│  │  │  ┌─────────────────────────────────────┐    │    │    │
│  │  │  │ FINITE (God the Supreme, humans)     │    │    │    │
│  │  │  │                                      │    │    │    │
│  │  │  │ ← Physics operates HERE              │    │    │    │
│  │  │  │ ← Most philosophy operates HERE      │    │    │    │
│  │  │  │ ← PhD focuses HERE but acknowledges  │    │    │    │
│  │  │  │   the higher levels exist             │    │    │    │
│  │  │  └─────────────────────────────────────┘    │    │    │
│  │  └─────────────────────────────────────────────┘    │    │
│  └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘    │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Each level CONTAINS all levels below it:**
- The absolute level contains the absonite and finite
- The absonite level contains the finite
- The infinite level contains all three

**Each level TRANSCENDS all levels below it:**
- The absonite transcends time/space (which the finite is bound by)
- The absolute transcends even the absonite's limits
- The infinite transcends all categories

## IMPLICATIONS FOR THE THEORY OF EVERYTHING

### A ToE of the finite level only is inherently incomplete
Physics-based ToE attempts operate at the finite level. The Foreword claims there are at least three more levels. A COMPLETE Theory of Everything must account for all four levels — or at least acknowledge that the finite-level theory is a subset of a larger framework.

### Consciousness operates across levels
The Foreword suggests that human consciousness, though primarily finite, can TOUCH higher levels:
- **Worship** and **wisdom** connect finite mind to spirit (which transcends the finite)
- **Cosmic consciousness** is awareness of the Supreme Being (the finite totality)
- **Mystical experience** may be contact with absonite or absolute realities

This is a specific, testable philosophical claim: if consciousness can access realities beyond the finite, then physicalism (which confines consciousness to the finite/material) is wrong.

### The four levels map to the four gravity circuits

| Level | Primary Gravity | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Finite | Physical gravity (Paradise) | Finite beings are primarily material |
| Absonite | Mind gravity (Infinite Spirit) | Absonite beings transcend matter; mind is their primary domain |
| Absolute | Spirit gravity (Eternal Son) | Absolute beings are primarily spiritual |
| Infinite | Personality gravity (Father) | The I AM is the source of personality |

**PhD finding:** This mapping suggests that as consciousness ascends through levels, it shifts from physical-gravity dominance to mind-gravity dominance to spirit-gravity dominance. The evolution of consciousness IS the progressive shift between gravity circuits.
