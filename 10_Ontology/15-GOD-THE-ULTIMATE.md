## KEY CONCEPT: "EVENTUATING"

The Ultimate is not "existing" (existential) and not "evolving" (experiential-finite). It is **eventuating** — a unique ontological status:

- Not yet fully actual (unlike the existential Deities)
- Not bound by time/space (unlike the finite Supreme)
- Coming into being in a way that transcends temporal sequence

**Analogy:** If the Supreme is like a tree GROWING (within time), the Ultimate is like a mathematical proof being DISCOVERED — it was always "there" in potential but becomes actual through a process that transcends temporal sequence.

## THE ABSONITE LEVEL

| Property | Finite | Absonite | Absolute |
|---|---|---|---|
| Time | Within | Transcended | Eternal |
| Space | Within | Transcended | Infinite |
| Origin | Has beginning | No beginning but not eternal | Unconditioned |
| Deity | Supreme | Ultimate | Absolute |
| Beings | Humans, angels | Architects, Transcendentalers | The three Deities |

The absonite is the bridge between finite experience and absolute eternity. It is the level at which the master universe is PLANNED by the Architects of the Master Universe.

## PhD IMPLICATIONS

**F41:** The absonite demonstrates that reality includes levels beyond spacetime — supporting the claim that consciousness (which touches the absonite through worship and wisdom) is not confined to physical spacetime. This is the strongest argument against physicalism from the Foreword: if there exist beings (Transcendentalers) whose consciousness operates entirely without spacetime, then consciousness is demonstrably NOT a product of spacetime processes.

**F42:** "Eventuating" as an ontological category has no parallel in Western philosophy. It is a genuine conceptual innovation — neither becoming (Hegelian) nor being (Parmenidean) but a third mode. This may warrant a section in Chapter 2.
