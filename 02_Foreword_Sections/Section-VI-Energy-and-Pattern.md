## KEY CONCEPTS

### Energy Hierarchy
The Foreword describes energy transmutations from primal to manifest:
1. **Paradise force** — pre-energy, not yet responsive to gravity
2. **Primordial force (puissant energy)** — first response to Paradise gravity
3. **Emergent energy (gravita)** — two phases: puissant and gravity-responding
4. **Universe power** — physical energy as we know it (atoms, particles)
5. **Mind energy** — distinct from physical energy, from the Infinite Spirit
6. **Spirit energy** — distinct from both, from the Eternal Son

### Pattern
Pattern is NOT a form of energy — it is the configuration that energy takes. Pattern is sourced from Paradise (which is the master pattern of all reality). Key properties:
- Independent of energy (same pattern can organize different energies)
- Bridges all three domains (physical patterns, mental patterns, spiritual patterns)
- Is "the master controller of energy" — energy follows pattern, not vice versa
