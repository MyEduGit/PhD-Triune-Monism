## KEY CONCEPTS

### The Seven Levels of Deity Approach
God the Sevenfold is the mechanism by which finite beings approach infinity:
1. Local universe Creator Sons (closest to finite creatures)
2. Ancients of Days (superuniverse rulers)
3. Seven Master Spirits (Paradise-universe bridge)
4. The Supreme Being (finite totality)
5. The Infinite Spirit (mind source)
6. The Eternal Son (spirit source)
7. The Universal Father (source of all)

### Significance for Philosophy of Mind
Consciousness has SEVEN LAYERS of deity-approach available to it. Mind does not merely exist — it exists WITHIN a structured hierarchy that extends from finite to infinite. This provides the ontological context for mind gravity's directedness.
