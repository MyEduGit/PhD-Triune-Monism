## KEY CONCEPTS

### God the Supreme
- The evolving Deity of the finite level
- NOT the Father (who is existential/eternal) — the Supreme is EXPERIENTIAL
- Grows through the experiences of all finite creatures
- Represents the totality of finite-level Deity achievement
- When complete, will unify all finite reality in one divine personality

### Significance for Consciousness
Consciousness is not merely personal — it is COSMIC. Every conscious experience contributes to the Supreme's growth. Mind is not isolated within individual skulls but participates in a cosmic process of deity actualization.
