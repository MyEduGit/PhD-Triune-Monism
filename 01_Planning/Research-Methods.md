## 1. PRIMARY METHOD: Comparative Philosophical Analysis

**Selected:** 2026-03-11 | **Score:** 8.0/10 | **Alternatives evaluated:** 5

The dissertation uses **comparative philosophical analysis** as its primary method, consisting of four integrated components:

### 1.1 Textual Analysis
- Close reading of the Foreword's 12 sections
- Extracting and formalizing implicit philosophical claims
- Direct citation with systematic interpretation
- **Applied in:** All 24 ontology files (Steps 1.1–6.3)

### 1.2 Conceptual Comparison
- Systematically mapping Foreword concepts onto existing philosophical positions
- 11 completed comparisons (Chalmers, Whitehead, IIT, Plotinus, Vedanta, Hegel, Spinoza, Kabbalah, Tillich, Physics/Standard Model, Physicalism)
- Weighted scoring across 8–10 criteria per comparison
- **Result:** Average Triune Monism advantage: +17.6% across all 11 comparisons

### 1.3 Argument Evaluation
- Testing logical validity and philosophical coherence using standard tools:
  - **Consistency** — does the framework contradict itself?
  - **Parsimony** — is it as simple as possible?
  - **Explanatory power** — does it explain what it claims to explain?
  - **Fertility** — does it generate new questions and predictions?
- **Applied in:** 05-THREE-DOMAINS (Mediator Argument), 08-FIRST-EQUATION (5-case test)

### 1.4 Dialectical Synthesis
- Show where existing frameworks fail (physicalism → hard problem; dualism → interaction problem; panpsychism → combination problem; IIT → no personality)
- Show where the Foreword's framework succeeds or fails
- **Applied in:** THEORY-OF-EVERYTHING-DRAFT, 05-THREE-DOMAINS, all comparison files

## 5. JUSTIFICATION FOR USING THE URANTIA BOOK AS A PHILOSOPHICAL SOURCE

**Anticipated objection:** "The Urantia Book is a religious text, not a philosophical one."

**Response (5 points):**
1. Many canonical philosophical works began as non-academic texts (Kierkegaard, Nietzsche, Wittgenstein)
2. The Foreword is *structurally* a philosophical system — defines terms, establishes categories, builds coherent ontology
3. Academic evaluation is of the *argument*, not the *source* — provenance is irrelevant to validity
4. Precedent: Whitehead's *Process and Reality* is treated as philosophy despite being deeply speculative and non-empirical
5. The dissertation does NOT ask readers to accept revelation — only to evaluate philosophical claims on their merits
