## THE SEVEN LEVELS

The Sevenfold is a STAIRCASE from finite creatures to the infinite Father:

| Level | Being | Function | Accessible to Humans? |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Creator Sons (e.g., Michael/Jesus) | Local universe sovereignty; personal revelation of the Father | YES — most directly |
| 2 | Ancients of Days | Superuniverse rule; justice and judgment | Indirectly |
| 3 | Seven Master Spirits | Govern seven superuniverses; channels of cosmic mind | Through cosmic intuitions |
| 4 | Supreme Being | The evolving finite totality | Through participation in evolution |
| 5 | Infinite Spirit | Third person of Trinity; source of mind | Through mind gravity |
| 6 | Eternal Son | Second person of Trinity; source of spirit | Through spirit gravity |
| 7 | Universal Father | First person of Trinity; source of personality | Through personality circuit; Thought Adjuster |

**Function:** Finite creatures cannot directly comprehend the infinite Father. The Sevenfold provides progressive levels of approach — each level more comprehensible to finite minds, each mediating the Father's nature.

## PhD IMPLICATIONS

The Sevenfold establishes that consciousness has a STRUCTURE of deity-approach — from material mind (level 1 contact through Creator Sons) through cosmic mind (level 3) to personality-Father contact (level 7). This implies consciousness has LAYERS corresponding to different gravity-circuit sensitivities, not just degrees of complexity.

**Finding F40:** Consciousness is layered (7 levels of deity-approach), not merely a spectrum of complexity. This challenges IIT's single-metric (phi) model.
