## KEY CONCEPTS

### Seven Absolutes of Infinity
The I AM self-distributes into seven functional aspects:

1. **The Universal Father** — First Person, source of personality and will
2. **The Eternal Son** — Second Person, source of spirit and spiritual gravity
3. **The Infinite Spirit** — Third Person, source of mind and mind gravity
4. **The Isle of Paradise** — Non-personal, source of physical gravity and pattern
5. **The Deity Absolute** — Deified potential, reservoir of unrealized deity possibilities
6. **The Unqualified Absolute** — Non-deified potential, reservoir of unrealized physical possibilities
7. **The Universal Absolute** — Unifier of deified and non-deified, maintains cosmic equilibrium

### Properties of the First Source and Center
- Infinite, eternal, absolute
- Self-willed, self-conscious, self-purposive
- Maintains personal relations with all personality
- Not limited by absoluteness — infinite will can self-limit
