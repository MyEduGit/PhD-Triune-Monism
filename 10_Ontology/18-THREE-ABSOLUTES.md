## The Three Absolutes

### DEITY ABSOLUTE
All possible deity/personal expression that has NOT YET been actualized. Every future person, every future spirit reality, every future divine expression is "stored" here in potential.

### UNQUALIFIED ABSOLUTE
All possible non-deity/physical expression that has NOT YET been actualized. Every future cosmic force, every future space configuration, every future material reality is "stored" here.

### UNIVERSAL ABSOLUTE
The compensator between the other two. It prevents infinite tension between deified and undeified potential. It ensures that the cosmos can be BOTH personal and impersonal, BOTH spiritual and material, without contradiction.

---

## THE FUNCTION IN THE TOE

The three Absolutes answer the question: **Where does new reality come from?**

Without them, the existing universe would be all there is — no growth, no evolution, no new creation. The Absolutes are the INFINITE RESERVOIR that ensures cosmic evolution can continue forever.

```
ACTUALS ←──[actualization]──── POTENTIALS
(what exists)                  (what can exist)
                                ├── Deity Absolute (new persons, spirits)
                                ├── Unqualified Absolute (new matter, force)
                                └── Universal Absolute (unity between them)
```

**PhD implication (F47):** The three Absolutes establish that reality is OPEN — not a closed system. This has implications for consciousness: if reality is open, consciousness may access genuinely new potentials that are not determined by prior physical states. This is the ontological ground for genuine free will (contra determinism).
