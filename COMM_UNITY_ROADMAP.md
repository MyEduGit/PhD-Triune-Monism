## RESEARCH PRINCIPLES

All research in this repository operates under UrantiOS:

### Truth

- Every claim cites its source (Urantia Book paper and section, or other primary source)
- Uncertainty is stated, never hidden
- When evidence contradicts a hypothesis, the hypothesis is revised
- Academic standards are honored — this is real scholarship, not advocacy

### Beauty

- Arguments are structured clearly: premise → evidence → conclusion
- Documents are well-organized: findable, readable, cross-referenced
- The framework is elegant: three domains, not seventeen
- Complexity is earned, not assumed

### Goodness

- This research serves the mission: spreading The Urantia Book
- But it also serves all truth-seekers, regardless of their connection to the Urantia Book
- Triune Monism is offered openly — not as proprietary doctrine, but as a contribution to human knowledge
- The PhD is not about Mircea's credentials — it is about making this framework academically legible
