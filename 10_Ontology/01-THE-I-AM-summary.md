## Definition
The **I AM** is the infinite, undivided reality that exists before all manifestation. It is the ultimate First Source and Center — an undifferentiated, self-existent unity. By hypothesis, the I AM is the unqualified Infinite whose self-will initiates the cosmos.

## Interpretation
The I AM is the **cosmological zero-point** — beyond space and time, utterly undifferentiated. It corresponds to the philosophical notion of the First Cause. "Undivided as in the Paradise Trinity" means the I AM is the singular unity behind all manifestations of God. The I AM is the **source matrix** out of which all reality (matter, mind, spirit) unfolds.

## Key Properties
- **Infinite & Unqualified:** No boundaries or conditions
- **Undifferentiated Unity:** Singular whole (infinite monad)
- **Self-Actualizing:** Contains inherent free will; originates differentiation by act of will
- **Primal Person:** Precedes personality but is revealed as the Universal Father in infinity
- **Transcendent:** Transcends all finite categories while underlying them

## Relationship to Differentiation
The I AM's self-division produces the Seven Absolutes (First Source and Center, Second Source, Third Source, Paradise, Deity Absolute, Universal Absolute, Unqualified Absolute). The I AM's act of will splits infinity into "actual" and "potential," giving rise to all reality.
