## KEY CONCEPTS

### Personality Properties
The Foreword specifies that personality:
1. Has SELF-CONSCIOUSNESS — awareness of itself as a self
2. Has relative FREE WILL — genuine choice capacity
3. Is UNIQUE — no two personalities are identical
4. Is UNCHANGING — personality does not change even as the being grows
5. Is UNIFYING — makes the three domains into ONE experiential being
6. Is bestowed by the FATHER alone

### Personality vs. Identity vs. Selfhood
- **Personality** — the unchanging capacity bestowed by the Father
- **Identity** — the particular configuration of personality + mind + body at a given time
- **Selfhood** — the subjective experience of being oneself
- **Character** — the cumulative result of personality's choices
- **Soul** — the jointly created midpoint between material and spiritual experience
