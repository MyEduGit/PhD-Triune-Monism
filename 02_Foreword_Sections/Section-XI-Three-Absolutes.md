## KEY CONCEPTS

### The Three Absolutes as Infinite Reservoirs
1. **Deity Absolute** — reservoir of all unrealized DEITY possibilities. Contains every potential divine expression not yet actualized.
2. **Unqualified Absolute** — reservoir of all unrealized NON-DEITY (physical) possibilities. Contains every potential physical configuration not yet actualized.
3. **Universal Absolute** — unifier of the other two Absolutes. Maintains equilibrium between deified and non-deified potential.

### Open System Ontology
The three Absolutes guarantee that reality is an OPEN SYSTEM — there is always more potential than actual. This is the ontological ground of FREE WILL (genuine novelty is possible because the future is not predetermined) and of EVOLUTION (growth toward actualization is always possible because potential is inexhaustible).
