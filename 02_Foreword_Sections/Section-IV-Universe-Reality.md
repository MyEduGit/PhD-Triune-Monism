## KEY CONCEPTS

### Reality Classification
Reality is either:
- **Actual** — existing, realized, experienceable
- **Potential** — not yet existing, in the three Absolutes

### Actual reality consists of:
- **MATTER** (things) — physical energy, sourced from Paradise
- **MIND** (meanings) — mental energy, sourced from the Infinite Spirit
- **SPIRIT** (values) — spiritual energy, sourced from the Eternal Son

### The Energy-Pattern-Personality Triad
Every actual thing is:
1. **Energy** — in one of three forms (physical, mindal, spiritual)
2. **Pattern** — the configuration/design that organizes energy
3. **Personality** — the unifying, self-conscious capacity (for beings that have it)

**THE MASTER EQUATION:** R = E x P x Psi (Reality = Energy x Pattern x Personality)
